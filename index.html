Welcome To NextLevelChess. The place to learn about Chess improvement & get your Chess to the Next Level!

I am GM Noël Studer, the youngest ever Swiss Chess Grandmaster. I won more than 10 Swiss Championship Titles (Classical, Rapid, Blitz & Team) and was a professional Chess Player from 2015-2021.

Now My Mission is to help Chess Lovers from ALL different Levels, Backgrounds, and Places to improve their Chess. That is why I have started this Chess Improvement Blog in March 2021.

I’m convinced that with the right mindset & tools, everybody can improve their Chess. My goal is to help you be the best version of yourself and reach your full potential.
